Our Story

A Place Where Dogs Truly Feel at Home

Founded in 2018, Petcare Spa began as a dream brought to life by Jalene with the help of her family — Ed, Michael, Halle — and their beloved rescues, Matzha Ball the Cockapoo and Sawyer the kitty.

Today, Petcare Spa is a 20,000-square-foot facility designed with dogs' comfort, safety, and happiness at the heart of every detail. Our team has grown to over 40 dedicated staff members.

Our high-flow air quality system equipped with UV sanitizing light eliminates 99.9% of viruses, bacteria, mold, and other pathogens.
